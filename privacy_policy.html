ToolScout Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

1. Information We Collect

ToolScout collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Product Information: Product titles, prices, and URLs from supported retail websites
  • Price Alerts: Your saved price alert preferences and settings
  • Usage Data: Basic extension usage statistics for improving functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Display product information and prices in the extension popup
  • Store and manage your price alerts
  • Provide price comparison functionality
  • Improve the extension's performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. We do not:

  • Transmit your personal data to external servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Track your browsing activity beyond supported retail sites

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally until you:

  • Manually delete price alerts through the extension interface
  • Uninstall the extension (which removes all stored data)
  • Clear your browser's extension data

5. Third-Party Websites

ToolScout operates on third-party retail websites to extract publicly available product information. We do not collect personal information from these sites beyond what is necessary for price comparison functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the extension
  • Delete individual price alerts or all stored data
  • Disable the extension at any time
  • Request information about data collection practices

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and communicated through extension updates.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the ToolScout extension, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@toolscout-extension.example.com

9. Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
